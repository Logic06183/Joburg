Methodology

Analysis follows the South African Weather Service (SAWS) definition of heat waves:

  • Heat wave threshold: 5°C above average summer maximum temperature
  • Requires 3+ consecutive days above threshold
  • Analysis period: Spring and Summer months (September-February)
  • Data source: ERA5 reanalysis dataset

Key Findings

  • Heat wave frequency: Increased from 3.0 to 7.0 events per season
  • Heat wave days: Doubled from 6.0 to 12.0 days per season
  • Maximum temperatures: Increased from 34.3°C to 37.4°C

About the Developer

Hi, I'm Craig Parker, a Data Scientist and Researcher at Wits Planetary Health Research. I specialize in environmental data analysis and visualization, focusing on the intersection of climate change and public health.

As Research Lead at Wits Planetary Health Research, my work centers on understanding environmental health impacts and maternal health outcomes. These visualizations were developed as part of our research into temperature patterns and their health impacts around Rahima Moosa Mother and Child Hospital. The analysis combines satellite data, statistical modeling, and interactive visualization techniques to make complex climate data more accessible and meaningful.
