DS-I Africa Profile Research Biography

These visualizations were developed for the Wits Planetary Health Research initiative, focusing on the environmental health impacts around Rahima Moosa Mother and Child Hospital. This work combines satellite data analysis, climate science, and public health research to understand the relationship between urban heat patterns and maternal health outcomes in Johannesburg.

Environmental and Health Visualizations
Rahima Moosa Hospital Study

A comprehensive analysis of temperature patterns and health outcomes around Rahima Moosa Mother and Child Hospital, combining historical data (1980-2023) with future projections to understand environmental health impacts in urban Johannesburg.
